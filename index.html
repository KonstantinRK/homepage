Welcome!

My name is Konstantin Kueffner and I am currently studying Logic and Computation at the Vienna University of Technology, where I am in the process of writing my masters thesis under the supervision of Agata Ciabattoni. Prior to persuing my master's degree, I completed my bachelor's degree at the Vienna University of Economics and Business, where I double majored in Business and Business Information Systems.
Currently, I am working as a teaching assistant for courses related to Knowledge Based Systems at the Vienna University of Technology and as a scientific assistant at the Vienna University of Economics and Business under Mark Strembeck. Priviously, I was a teaching assistant for the courses Networks, Network Security, Data Science at the Vienna University of Economics and Business. Moreover, my latest non-academic position was an Internship at the Austrian Parialment.

Lastly, in no particular order, my current interests are in the areas of

  • Non-Classical Logics,
  • Complexity Theory,
  • Logic and Games and
  • Automated Reasoning.
